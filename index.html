Bridges of Culture: Brazil and Germany through my lens

About

This project showcases a collection of photos from my travels across Brazil, contrasted with images from my visit to Germany, where I attended the Euro Cup matches and immersed myself in the local language and culture. Through this visual journey, I highlight the diverse landscapes, experiences, and contrasts between these two fascinating countries. It’s a personal exploration of travel, culture, and language.

Brazil

This section of the project features photos taken in Brasília, São Paulo, and the Fernando de Noronha archipelago. The images capture the vibrant urban landscapes of Brasília and São Paulo, alongside the breathtaking natural beauty of Noronha’s pristine beaches and wildlife. Each photo reflects the unique essence of these iconic Brazilian locations.

Germany

This part of the project showcases photographs taken in Berlin and Munich, highlighting the blend of history and modernity in both cities. From Berlin's iconic landmarks like the Brandenburg Gate to Munich’s charming architecture and vibrant culture, the images reflect the rich heritage and dynamic atmosphere of Germany.
